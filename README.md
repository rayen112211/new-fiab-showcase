## THE ART OF PRECISION

**NEW FIAB** stands at the intersection of traditional Italian craftsmanship and cutting-edge precision engineering. We specialize in the development and production of high-end metal components for the world's most prestigious luxury fashion houses.

From intricate buckles and clasps to complex mechanical hardware, every component we produce is a testament to our dedication to micron-level accuracy and flawless aesthetic finishing.

